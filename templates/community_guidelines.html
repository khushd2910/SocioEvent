Community Guidelines

These guidelines help keep our platform safe, respectful, and trustworthy for everyone.

Respect All Users
Treat event organizers, attendees, and staff with respect at all times.
No Fake or Misleading Events
Creating misleading, duplicate, or fraudulent events may result in account suspension.
Accurate Event Information
Ensure event date, time, location, pricing, and descriptions are correct and up to date.
No Harassment or Abuse
Harassment, hate speech, or abusive behavior is strictly prohibited on the platform.
Fair Ticket Usage
Tickets must not be resold, duplicated, or misused outside the platform.
Privacy & Data Protection
Personal information must never be shared, misused, or collected without consent.
No Platform Misuse
Automated scraping, spamming, or abusing platform features is not allowed.
Intellectual Property
Do not upload copyrighted images, logos, or content without proper rights.
Event Cancellations & Changes
Organizers are responsible for informing attendees of cancellations or changes.
Enforcement Notice: Violations of these guidelines may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans. These guidelines may be updated periodically to improve platform safety.
